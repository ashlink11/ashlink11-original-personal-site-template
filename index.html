BACKGROUND

Since I was very young, I was always concerned with meaning, truth, and consciousness.

When I was 18 years old, I learned about Moore’s Law and the technological singularity calculated to be coming in the year 2045. The philosophical concept of exponentiality fascinated me, as well as the idea of making the world a better place with the Internet.

These days, I am grateful to simply code and be a small part of the open-source movement.

I have a background in computer science, the startup world, freelance website management, as well as full-stack app development. I also love teaching and tutoring.

I feel empowered to be able to create nearly anything I can dream up. I’m grateful to all my teachers and friends along the way.
